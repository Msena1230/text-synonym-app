Privacy Policy / プライバシーポリシー

English

Effective Date: October 30, 2025

SynonymFinderPro (hereinafter referred to as "the developer") establishes the following privacy policy (hereinafter referred to as "this Policy") regarding the handling of user information in the application "Synonym Finder" (hereinafter referred to as "the Service").

Article 1 (General Provisions)

To protect user information, the developer shall comply with the Personal Information Protection Act of Japan, guidelines from relevant ministries, and other related laws and regulations, and shall handle user information appropriately in accordance with this Policy.

Article 2 (Information Collected and Purposes of Use)

In providing the Service, we lawfully and fairly collect the following information and use it within the scope of the respective purposes of use.

  1. User Authentication Information
    • Information Collected: Information provided by Google upon authentication using a Google Account (name, email address, Google Account ID).
    • Purpose of Use: For user authentication, account management, and data synchronization within the Service.
  2. Confidential Information Saved by the User in the Service
    • Information Collected: (a) Text data entered by the user and saved in the Service, bookmarked words, and learning history. (b) API keys voluntarily configured by the user (e.g., Google Gemini API key, Sapling AI API key).
    • Purpose of Use: To provide cloud synchronization and all features of the Service. This information is stored in a Firebase database.
  3. Technical Information Associated with Service Use
    • Information Collected: Device information, OS information, IP address, service operation logs, error logs, and cookies.
    • Purpose of Use: For the stable provision and maintenance of the Service, ensuring security, monitoring performance, statistical analysis of usage, and for service improvement and bug fixing.

Article 3 (Strict Restrictions on Access to Confidential Information)

For user convenience (cloud synchronization), the Service stores confidential information as defined in Article 2, Section 2 (API keys and saved text) in a Firebase database.

Although we are technically capable of accessing this data on the system, we prioritize user privacy and security. We will never intentionally view, copy, use, or disclose the content of a user's confidential information to any third party, except in the following exceptional cases and only when required by law or with the user's explicit consent (e.g., for a support request).

  • When there is a specific support request from the user: We may access the information to the minimum extent necessary to resolve the issue, after obtaining the user's consent to do so.
  • In the event of a critical system failure or security breach investigation: When technical verification is reasonably deemed unavoidable for the protection of the entire Service and after following legally required procedures.
  • When a disclosure request is made based on laws or legal proceedings: When we receive a formal inquiry or disclosure order from a public authority such as a court or the police, based on law.

Article 4 (Security Measures)

We utilize advanced security features provided by Firebase (such as strict access control to the database and SSL/TLS encryption for communications) to prevent the leakage, loss, or damage of collected user information (especially API keys and saved text). Furthermore, we implement technically feasible encryption measures when storing user API keys in the database.

Article 5 (Provision to Third Parties)

Except as stipulated by law or in Article 3, the developer will not provide personal information to third parties without the prior consent of the user.

Article 6 (Provision of Information to Third-Party Services)

To provide the core functions of the Service, the developer shares user information with the following third-party services. The handling of information by these services is governed by their respective privacy policies. Please review them for more details.

  • Firebase (Google LLC): Used for user authentication, database, and hosting of the Service. Google Privacy Policy
  • Google AI Platform / Gemini (Google LLC): Used for synonym suggestions and text humanization features. Text entered by the user is sent to this service for processing. Google Privacy Policy
  • Sapling (Sapling Intelligence, Inc.): Used for the AI content detection feature. Text entered by the user is sent to this service for processing. Sapling Privacy Policy

Article 7 (Data Retention Period and Deletion)

If a user deletes their account for the Service, or if the Service has not been used for [e.g., one year] or more, the developer will endeavor to promptly delete the collected user information (including API keys, saved text, etc.).

Article 8 (Changes to this Policy)

The developer may revise this Policy in response to changes in laws, service content, etc. The revised policy will be notified by posting it on the Service or on the developer's website, and it will become effective from the time of posting.

Article 9 (Governing Law and Jurisdiction)

The governing law for this Policy shall be the laws of Japan. Any disputes arising in connection with this Policy shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the Tokyo District Court in the first instance.

Article 10 (Contact Information)

For inquiries regarding this Policy, please contact us at the following:

  • Operating Name: SynonymFinderPro
  • Contact: synonymfinderpro@gmail.com

(As this is an individually developed service, responses to inquiries may take time, and we cannot guarantee a response to all inquiries.)
